The settlement of the title fight was here! It may not be too much of a risk to say that the Bangladesh Premier League title is once again going to Bashundhara Kings. Because after today’s match, Abahani Limited has been knocked out of the fight.

Kings have never lost to Abahani in Premier League history. The current champions continue that trend. 2-0 win in the most high-voltage match of the season. As a result of this loss, the gap between Abahani and Kings is now 8 points. Kings have secured the top spot with full 15 points in five matches. Abahani is third with 7 points. For them, hoping for a title is now a daydream.
